Discord ChatBot

Topics: TypeScript, JavaScript, Node.js, Discord.js

Concept

I used this as my graduation project, and it was my first "big" project. It is quite simple on the outside, but involves a lot of concepts and code. This project helped me understand how to work with APIs and web, databases, users needs / feedbacks, and more.

Discord is a messaging platform used mainly on computer, that allows to hang out with your friends via text or voice chat and offers a module (written in different languages) that can be used to create a ChatBot with their free API.

The bot implements a lot of different functionalities, such as browsing Reddit directly from the Discord chat, listening to music from Spotify, Youtube and files, images manipulation with DeepAI APIs, and more.
